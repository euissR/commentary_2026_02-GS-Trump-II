US Military Strikes Over Time

This visualization shows US military strikes from 2017 to 2025, stacked by week and location. Each dot represents a strike event.

Temporal Patterns

The dot plot reveals temporal clustering of strikes, with certain weeks showing concentrated military activity. Different colors represent different countries.

Geographic Distribution

World Map View

Regional Focus

Final View

IEEPA Tariff Categories

Countries subject to IEEPA tariffs are categorized by the type of tariff policy applied. This map shows the different categories of reciprocal tariffs, exemptions, and special cases.

Policy Categories

Five distinct categories emerge: reciprocal tariffs, exempt nations, free speech limitations, fentanyl crisis responses, and Russian oil secondary tariffs. Each represents a different US trade policy approach.

Tariff Rates

Now let's look at the actual tariff rates applied to each country. The map transitions to show a continuous scale, revealing the range of tariff percentages from lowest to highest.

Rate Distribution

The continuous color scale highlights the variation in tariff rates, from minimal rates (lighter colors) to substantial tariffs (darker colors), showing the differentiated approach to trade policy.

Global Impact

This dual view—categorical and continuous—reveals both the policy framework and its quantitative implementation across different nations under IEEPA authority.
