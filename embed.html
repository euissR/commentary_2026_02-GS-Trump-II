Peace Agreements and Resource Interests

Peace agreements brokered during this period often include resource-sharing components, particularly around critical minerals.

India-Pakistan

May have paved the way for MoU between Pakistan FWO and US Strategic Minerals company in September.

Israel-Iran (12 Day War)

Peace deal reached with no minerals component.

DRC - Rwanda

The "Critical Minerals for Security and Peace Deal" seeks to stabilize eastern DRC and facilitate cooperation over rare earth mineral extraction.

Armenia - Azerbaijan

Establishes the "Trump Route for International Peace and Prosperity" (TRIPP) with 99-year development rights for transit corridor.

Israel-Hamas

Includes Trump economic development plan for Gaza with special economic zone provisions.

Cambodia-Thailand

US signed MoUs on Diversifying Global Critical Minerals Supply Chains with both Malaysia and Thailand.

Ukraine-Russia

Proposed development fund includes technology, data centers, and rare earth metal extraction projects in the Arctic.

Serbia-Kosovo

No peace deal reached. No war in 2025.

Egypt-Ethiopia Renaissance Dam

Dispute remains unresolved. No war in 2025.

Policy Categories

Five distinct categories emerge: reciprocal tariffs, exempt nations, free speech limitations, fentanyl crisis responses, and Russian oil secondary tariffs. Each represents a different US trade policy approach.

Tariff Rates

Now let's look at the actual tariff rates applied to each country. The map transitions to show a continuous scale, revealing the range of tariff percentages from lowest to highest.

Rate Distribution

Global Impact

US-EU Trade 2017-2025

This chart shows monthly trade volumes between the US and EU across different product categories from 2017 to 2025. Each horizontal bar represents a month, with colors showing different trade categories.

Trade Evolution

Over the years, trade patterns have shifted significantly. Manufactured goods and machinery consistently dominate, while raw materials show negative balances (imports exceed exports).

Focus on 2025

Let's zoom into 2025 to see the dramatic impact of tariff negotiations. The chart will expand to show month-by-month detail with a smoother area representation.

2025 Trade Timeline

Throughout 2025, a series of tariff announcements, negotiations, and retaliatory measures shaped trade volumes. The annotations on the right mark key events in this ongoing trade dispute.

Key Turning Points

Notice the trade fluctuations corresponding to major announcements: Liberation Day tariffs in April, the 90-day pause, threats of 50% tariffs, and finally the July trade deal establishing a 15% ceiling.

Resolution Path

By late 2025, implementation dialogues and formal negotiations showed progress toward stabilizing the trade relationship, though volumes remained below pre-tariff levels.
